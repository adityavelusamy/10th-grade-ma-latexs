\section{}
Gallian Ch 3.30:\\
Prove that the dihedral group of order 6 does not have a subgroup of order 4.\\
\begin{proof}
Say we $H$ is the subgroup of $D_3$ of order 4. If $\rho\in H$, there are too many elements in $H$. If $\rho \not\in H$, then $H = \{Id.,\tau ,\tau\rho, \tau\rho^2\}.$ However, $\tau \cdot \tau\rho = \rho$ but $\rho \not\in H$. Therefore, $H$ is not a subgroup of $D_3$ with order 4.
\end{proof}
